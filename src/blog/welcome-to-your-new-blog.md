# Welcome to Your New Blog!

This is your first blog post. You can edit this post or delete it and start creating your own content.

## Getting Started with Blog Posts

When you're ready to create your first post:

1. Access the CMS at `/admin`
2. Click "New Post" under the Blog collection
3. Fill in all the required fields
4. Upload a featured image
5. Write your content in Markdown
6. Click "Publish" when ready

## Tips for Great Blog Posts

- **Write compelling headlines** that grab attention
- **Use images** to make your posts more engaging
- **Break up text** with headings and short paragraphs
- **Include calls-to-action** to encourage reader engagement
- **Optimize for SEO** with relevant keywords and meta descriptions
